Our Values

We are built on four core values. They will always be evident and exercised. That is our promise to you.

Integrity

Integrity is our cornerstone and lead. We believe in honesty, empathy, and showing a consistent and uncompromising adherence to strong moral and ethical principles and values. We are proud to be built on a foundation of integrity, not just in word, but in action.

Customer Commitment

Our employees are invaluable to us. They are the backbone of everything we do. They are challenged, developed, and appreciated, but most importantly, a vital part of a company making a difference in the world. We believe in our employees, their families, and their futures.

Employee Well-Being

Meeting customer expectations is not good enough. Our company culture begins at exceeding expectations. From exceptional quality and customer service to product guarantees and customer appreciation events, we listen, deliver value, and believe in earning customer approval every day.

Executional Excellence

Executional excellence is an important value for us. It is needed to drive innovation and growth and meet obligations to customers, employees, and shareholders. This is achieved by using techniques to move from strategy to tactics.

Our Founder

I know what it feels like to suffer from cold sores. I have dealt with them my entire life. When I was a teenager, I remember the shame, embarrassment, bullying, and disparaging comments every time I had a cold sore. As I grew older, more of the same; two-three cold sores every year. I tried over-the-counter products, drug prescriptions, and home remedies without success. In the office or a meeting, I felt like all eyes were on my cold sore. I remember taking sick days to allow healing because it felt terrible and depressing to have a disgusting sore on my lip. In 2010, I decided I was not going to live like this anymore. The year after year suffering, pain, and embarrassment was going to end. Through scientific literature mining and I founded So-B with the belief that no one should have to live their life with cold sores. No one should have to suffer like I did or worse. People should have the ability to live their life on their terms, carefree, without the worry of cold sores.

Robert Hall Founder & CEO
